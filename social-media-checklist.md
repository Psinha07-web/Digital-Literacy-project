# Social Media Checklist for College Students
**Responsible Digital Communication — Do's and Don'ts**

---

## ✅ Do's

- **Think before you post** — Ask yourself: would you be comfortable if your professor or future employer saw this?
- **Keep your profiles professional** — Use a clear profile photo and appropriate bio, especially on LinkedIn.
- **Give credit** — Always credit original creators when sharing or reposting their content.
- **Use privacy settings** — Regularly review who can see your posts and personal information.
- **Fact-check before sharing** — Verify information from reliable sources before spreading it online.
- **Be respectful in comments and DMs** — Treat others online the same way you would in person.

---

## ❌ Don'ts

- **Don't share personal details publicly** — Avoid posting your phone number, address, or daily schedule openly.
- **Don't post in anger** — Avoid responding to conflicts online impulsively; take time to cool down first.
- **Don't plagiarise or misrepresent** — Never pass off someone else's work, quotes, or ideas as your own.
- **Don't badmouth institutions or people** — Complaining about your college, professors, or classmates online can seriously backfire.
- **Don't overshare** — Constant personal updates can hurt your professional image and compromise your privacy.
- **Don't ignore cyberbullying** — If you witness it, report it. If it happens to you, seek support — don't stay silent.
